Spanish Omelette

Description

It looks like a pie made with thinly sliced potatoes & onions.

Ingredients

  • Eggs - 3
  • Potatoes - 400gm
  • Onion - 150gm
  • Extra virgin olive oil - 80ml
  • Salt & Pepper - to taste

Steps

  1. Thinly slice the onions & potatoes about 3 mm thick.
  2. Fry them in extra virgin olive oil in a pan on high heat for 10 to 15 mins until golden.
  3. Drain the vegetables & save the olive oil for later use.
  4. In a bowl, whisk the eggs, then add the vegetables, salt and pepper, mix it then leave it to set for 15 to 20 mins.
  5. In a nonstick pan, pour the egg mixture & fry on high heat for 1 min, then lower the heat and cook for another 3 - 4 mins.
  6. Cover the pan with large plate & carefully turn the omelette onto the pan.
  7. Slide the omelette back in the pan & fry the other side for 3 - 4 mins.
  8. Poke the knife in the center if it comes out clean then it's done.
  9. Repeat step 6 to remove the omelette and serve it.
